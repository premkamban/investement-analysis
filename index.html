Notes & assumptions: Loan EMI uses reducing-balance amortisation. Lease comparison assumes the lease rental bundles insurance & maintenance (typical for corporate/fleet leases) — for an apples-to-apples view, add your expected annual insurance + maintenance to the loan side mentally, or factor it into the on-road price. Tax savings under the salaried route use Income-tax Rules perquisite valuation for employer-provided cars (a flat monthly value added to taxable salary, far lower than actual EMI, since the car is owned & depreciated by the employer); under the business/self-employed route, loan interest + 15% p.a. depreciation on the car are claimed as business expenses against profits, so the tax saved depends on your marginal slab. This is an illustrative planning tool, not tax advice — please confirm with a CA before structuring your compensation or claiming deductions.
